Women in AI & Analytics Mentoring — Empowering the Next Generation of AI and Analytics
We are a nonprofit community dedicated to elevating women in the fields of Artificial Intelligence and Analytics through mentorship, collaboration, and knowledge sharing. Our mission is to foster a supportive network where aspiring and established professionals connect, grow, and thrive in their careers.
🌟 What We Do:
•	Match mentees with experienced mentors in AI, data science, and analytics
•	Host educational webinars, workshops, and networking events
•	Create opportunities for skill development, career growth, and leadership
•	Champion diversity and inclusion in tech and data-driven industries
Whether you're just beginning your journey or have years of experience to share, this is your space to build meaningful connections and shape the future of AI and analytics with purpose and passion.
